The brand Skittles founded in 1974 by a British Company. At the time they were called "Glees". Since then Skittles have become the second most popular candies in America. They have a variety of different fruity flavors. Some of these flavors include strawberry, grape, orange, lemon, lime, and green apple. These yummy candies are even acceptable for vegitarian and vegans. They have a chewy center with a crunchy outer shell. No one is quite sure who invented the skittles. Skittles are a very loved candy throughout many places.
